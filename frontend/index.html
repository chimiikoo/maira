Our Services

Manicure

No polish, Minimalist Care, Maximum Clean

A Russian-style dry manicure using e-file techniques to shape, smooth, and refresh your nails without polish. Promotes healthy growth and keeps your hands perfectly groomed and fresh.

Russian Manicure with Hard Gel

Strength Meets Beauty

This reinforced manicure uses strengthening base layers and premium gel polish for added durability, protection, and shine. Lasts for 3.5-4 weeks and more.

Russian Gel Extension

Precision Sculpted

Precision sculpted extensions that add length and structure, creating natural-looking nails with a smooth, durable finish.

Smart Gel Pedicure

Smooth. Strong. Stunning.

This dry e-file pedicure deeply cleanses and reshapes your feet, removes buildup, and finishes with a hydrating mousse for soft, camera-ready toes.

KART Pedicure

Treatment-Focused

More than a regular pedicure — it's a treatment-focused service designed to safely and effectively improve the condition of the feet without aggressive filing.

Japanese Naked Manicure

Ancient Technique

An ancient Japanese technique that involves buffing nutrients and healthy minerals deep into the nail bed, this manicure requires no polish. Perfect for anyone who wants their hands to look meticulously groomed and natural.
